WHAT DOES “UI” STAND FOR?


U- unprivileged

Our service is designed to support students who may be financially constrained but still desire academic guidance.


I- international

Our tutoring service is specifically designed to help students who are non-native English speakers. We offer tutoring in multiple languages, creating an opportunity for students to improve their language skills and gain effective tutoring.
